About Us

At Extreme Tourism by Aiman Nabil, we believe that the best way to experience the beauty of nature is to immerse yourself in it. Our tours take you off the beaten path and allow you to explore the wilderness in a way that few people ever get to experience.

We are a team of experienced guides who are passionate about adventure and the outdoors. We have explored some of the most remote and stunning locations in the world and we want to share those experiences with you.

Our tours are designed to challenge you physically and mentally, while also allowing you to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of your surroundings. Whether you are an experienced adventurer or a first-time explorer, we have a tour that is right for you.

Our Tours

We offer a wide variety of extreme activity for thrill-seekers of all difficulty. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a first-time adventurer, we have something for you.

  1. Hiking Tour


    • Explore stunning natural landscapes on foot
    • Challenge yourself with varying degrees of difficulty and terrain
    • Experience the local flora and fauna up close and personal
    • Learn about the cultural and historical significance of the area from knowledgeable guides

  2. Kayaking Tour


    • Discover hidden coves and secluded beaches
    • Paddle through crystal clear waters and see marine life up close
    • Enjoy the tranquility of being out on the open sea
    • Experience the thrill of navigating rapids and whitewater

  3. Rock Climbing Tour


    • Challenge yourself physically and mentally with vertical ascents and descents
    • Learn valuable skills such as knot tying, belaying, and rappelling
    • Experience the breathtaking views from atop mountain peaks and cliffs
    • Get a rush of adrenaline as you conquer challenging routes and obstacles

  4. Bungee Jumping Tour


    • Experience the ultimate thrill of jumping from a high platform or bridge
    • Feel the rush of freefalling before the bungee cord catches you
    • Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding area during your jump
    • Experience a one-of-a-kind adrenaline rush that you'll never forget

  5. Snorkeling Tour


    • Explore the vibrant underwater world of coral reefs and marine life
    • Experience the thrill of swimming with tropical fish and sea turtles
    • Learn about the ecosystem and conservation efforts from knowledgeable guides
    • Enjoy the crystal-clear waters and stunning natural beauty of the area

  6. Fishing Tour


    • Experience the thrill of catching a variety of fish species in their natural habitat
    • Learn about local fishing techniques and traditions from experienced guides
    • Enjoy the tranquility of being out on the open water
    • Take home your catch for a fresh and delicious meal

  7. Skydiving Tour


    • Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush of jumping out of a plane at high altitude
    • Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape during your freefall
    • Experience the tranquility of floating under a parachute before landing
    • Receive training and guidance from experienced instructors to ensure your safety

  8. Skiing Tour


    • Experience the thrill of skiing down snowy slopes in breathtaking mountain scenery
    • Learn or improve your skiing skills with experienced instructors
    • Enjoy the fresh mountain air and stunning views
    • Choose from a variety of ski runs suitable for all skill levels

  9. Gliding Tour


    • Experience the thrill of soaring through the air in a glider
    • Enjoy the breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape from a unique perspective
    • Learn about the principles of gliding and how to control the glider from experienced pilots
    • Experience the tranquility of gliding silently through the air with only the sound of the wind

  10. Surfing Tour


    • Ride the waves and experience the thrill of surfing on some of the best beaches in the world
    • Learn or improve your surfing skills from experienced instructors
    • Enjoy the sun, sand, and surf lifestyle
    • Choose from a variety of surfing locations suitable for all skill levels
